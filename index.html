Devora Avila is currently a third-year student at UC Riverside studying Media & Cultural Studies. After taking an intro class to her major, she became fascinated and passionate about learning the impact of Media has in our society. In her studies, she has acquired a 3.8 GPA and is involved in campus life. She is a member of a campus club, Underground Scholars, and attends regular meetings. Underground Scholars supports people affected by the incarceration system and constantly holds community events. Outside of school, she is a part of an internship program, called Avalon Fashion Group, where she gets the opportunity to speak with experts in the fashion industry and provide her opinions regarding fashion trends. Each month, she collects images of fashion trends and constructs a well-made collage with all her findings acquired from various social media platforms, including Instagram, Pinterest, and Tiktok. She also responds to frequent data questionnaires, such as polls or google forms.

As a young girl, Devora wished to become a doctor when she grew up. She wanted to help people and believed becoming a doctor was the easiest way to help people in the world. As she grew older and came to know herself better, she realized that becoming a doctor was not for her. However, she still wanted to help other people and make a difference in the world. Of course, everyone wishes to make a mark in the world; but, Devora was serious about making a difference. Growing up, Devora did not see lots of representation in the media. In the media, she saw most opportunities to showcase lighter skin tones and individuals of a white background. For this reason, she wishes to become involved in the public relations field. Devora believes she can be a great help to making the public media a more welcoming place to all people. She believes by working in public relations, she can make significant decisions to construct more inclusive projects. Then, children of color growing up can finally grow to see representation where they feel included and seen.
